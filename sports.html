Sports

Driven by ambition

Football has shaped who I am—on and off the pitch. It teaches me discipline, focus, and how to handle pressure. I approach every match with intensity and purpose, always looking to improve and support the team. Whether in training or competition, I stay adaptable, coachable, and committed to growing as a player.

I have competed in a variety of prestigious sports programs and tournaments, including GBAC and FOBISIA. These experiences have honed my skills, discipline, and competitive spirit, pushing me to excel both individually and as part of a team. Participating in these events has been fundamental to my growth as an athlete and footballer.
