About

Kittinat Gerdsri

I'm a multidisciplinary creator blending technology, design, and structured thinking. With a foundation in mathematics, sciences, and languages, I approach problem-solving with clarity and logic — applied through AI, coding, and robotics to build intelligent, real-world systems.

Alongside this, I express ideas visually through graphic design, drawing, 3D design, animation, and photography, focusing on clean and purposeful outputs. I value teamwork and collaboration as key drivers of innovation. Sports, especially athletics and football, build my discipline and resilience, while piano strengthens my creativity and focus.

Ultimately, I aim to create work that connects creativity and engineering — simple, functional, and meaningful.
