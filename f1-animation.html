Project 01 · Version 2

F1 Animation

Speed, sculpted frame by frame

A complete Formula 1 concept car built from scratch in Blender — modeled, textured with a custom livery, rigged for suspension and steering, and animated into cinematic shots. This project taught me the full 3D pipeline: from blocking out geometry to lighting a scene, animating a camera, and rendering a finished sequence.
