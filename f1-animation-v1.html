F1 Animation — Version 2023

Where it started

The first version of the F1 Animation project, built in 2023 — my first complete attempt at modeling, texturing, and animating a Formula 1 car in Blender. Everything I learned here, from camera work to livery design, fed directly into the rebuilt version 2 of the project.
